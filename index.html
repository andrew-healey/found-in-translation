Found in Translation

The game where Google Translate butchers your sentences!

How to play:

  1. Enter some text into the box marked 'Input text...'. Try famous quotes.
  2. Choose a number of times that Google will process your sentence, from 10 to 30
  3. Click 'Butcher' to butcher the sentence
  4. Watch as Google cycles through some of its hundreds of languages
  5. If it doesn't work, Google has found you and knows that you are a bot. Wait 15 minutes and try again later.
